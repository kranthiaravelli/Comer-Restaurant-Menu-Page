Comer Restaurant
Menu Page

Comer Restaurant is a fine dining for everyone. Built on the belief that luxury should not be a privilege, Comer offers premium-quality dishes at affordable prices, making world-class flavors accessible to the heart of every community. With a menu inspired by global cuisine and crafted using high-quality ingredients, Comer bridges the gap between gourmet dining and affordability. From elegantly plated main courses to refined desserts and beverages, every dish is served with the finesse of a five-star experience — without the five-star price tag.

At its core, Comer champions a dedicated mission:

“To ensure that people from all walks of life — especially the middle class — can experience the joy of premium food, without compromise.” Elegant yet welcoming, Comer isn’t just a place to eat — it’s a movement that celebrates dignity, inclusivity, and flavor.

Starters

Seafood Starters (any dish - $27)

  1. Seared Scallops

    Lightly pan-seared scallops served on a bed of purée (e.g., pea or cauliflower), often with a citrus or beurre blanc sauce.

  2. Tuna Tartare

    Finely diced raw tuna mixed with avocado, herbs, and light seasoning, often served with microgreens or on a crisp base.

  3. Oysters Rockefeller

    Baked oysters topped with herbs, breadcrumbs, and a rich buttery sauce.

  4. Lobster Bisque

    A velvety, creamy soup made from lobster stock, wine, and aromatics, often garnished with lobster meat.

Meat-Based Starters (any dish - $35)

  1. Beef Carpaccio

    Paper-thin raw beef slices served with arugula, parmesan shavings, and a drizzle of olive oil or truffle oil.

  2. Duck Liver Parfait (Foie Gras)

    Smooth, rich liver mousse served with brioche toast and sweet accompaniments like fig jam or pear compote.

  3. Lamb Koftas

    Spiced minced lamb skewers, served with yogurt sauce or harissa and herbs.

Cheese & Dairy Starters (any dish - $15)

  1. Baked Camembert

    Whole Camembert cheese baked until gooey, often paired with honey, nuts, and toasted sourdough.

  2. Burrata with Heirloom Tomatoes

    Creamy burrata cheese served with colorful tomatoes, basil, and a balsamic reduction.

Vegetarian Starters (any dish - $18)

  1. Stuffed Zucchini Blossoms

    Delicate zucchini flowers filled with ricotta or goat cheese, lightly battered and fried or served fresh.

  2. Wild Mushroom Arancini

    Crispy risotto balls filled with wild mushrooms and cheese, served on a tomato or truffle aioli base.

  3. Charred Asparagus with Hollandaise

    Grilled asparagus spears served with a silky hollandaise sauce and sometimes topped with a poached quail egg.
